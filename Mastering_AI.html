Mastering AI with Custom GPT's





In grand technical event of SSGMCE “PERSUIT’24” the ISTE Student's Chapter of Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering, Shegaon successfully hosted a “Mastering AI with Custom GPT” Workshop on 16th march 2024 . This workshop was open to first-year students from all departments, and a total of 49 students participated with great enthusiasm. The program featured valuable contributions from the Principal, Respected S. B. Somani Sir, the esteemed Resource person Mr. Akshay Agrawal Sir, software engineer at Wipro, Pune. Head of the ASH Department, Dr. A. S. Tale Sir, along with the Faculty Coordinator of ISTE, Dr. M.S. Pande Mam, and Faculty Advisor of ISTE, Prof. H.B. Patil Sir, offered valuable insights and encouraged students to actively participate in future events and workshops. The workshop began with the welcoming of dignitaries and the inauguration ceremony. Following the schedule, the event consisted of various activities spanning ten hours. In the end, the event concluded successfully
