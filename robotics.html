Robotics Workshop







The ISTE Student Chapter organized a 13-hour "ROBOTICS WORKSHOP" on September 16th and 17th, 2023, with 87 first-year student participants from various departments. The workshop aimed to promote teamwork, foster a robotics and STEM culture, provide hands-on experience, encourage innovation, and enhance problem-solving skills. It successfully achieved these objectives, improving students' robotics understanding and technical abilities while inspiring their interest in robotics-related pursuits. The event featured prominent resource persons and concluded with a Valedictory Function, positively impacting the college's robotics community.
