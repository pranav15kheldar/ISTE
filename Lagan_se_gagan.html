Lagan Se Gagan


"Lagan Se Gagan" on Women's Day was an empowering event that aimed to inspire women to reach for the stars and break barriers. Held at the Swadhaya Kaksha, the event brought together women from various walks of life to commemorate International Women's Day. Throughout the day, attendees were treated to inspiring keynote addresses from notable speaker Mrs. Rupal Mohota who is Mrs. India Universe Actress and Singer shared their journeys of overcoming challenges and achieving success. Engaging panel discussions delved into important topics such as entrepreneurship, leadership, and challenging gender stereotypes, sparking insightful conversations among participants. Interactive event provided valuable opportunities for skill-building and personal development, empowering women with the tools they need to thrive in their endeavors. The event also celebrated the remarkable achievements of women across different fields, serving as a source of inspiration for all. Networking sessions facilitated meaningful connections and collaborations, fostering a supportive community of women empowering women. Cultural performances added vibrancy to the event, celebrating the diversity and creativity of women from various backgrounds. As the event concluded, attendees were energized and motivated to continue championing gender equality and supporting one another on their journey to success.
