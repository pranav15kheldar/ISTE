Latex





In accordance with the stated objective, the ISTE Student's Chapter of Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering, Shegaon has successfully hosted a Hands on Workshop on “LATEX Tool for Technical Writing” on 16th & 17th Sept. 2023, open to all the Students and Faculty Members. Total 22 students enthusiastically participated in this workshop. Faculty Advisor, Prof. H.B. Patil Sir and Faculty Coordinator, Mrs. A.A. Deshmukh Mam given the note of the program and motivated students to participate in future events
ISTE Faculty Advisor, Prof. H. B. Patil Sir and Faculty Coordinator, Mrs. A.A. Deshmukh Mam welcomed ISTE Secretary, Dr. R. S. Dhekekar Sir and Resource Persons Dr. R.S. Mahamune Sir and Dr.Neerja Dharmale Madam, the Faculties of Electronics and Telecommunication Department, and requested them to inaugurate the program. Mrs. A.A. Deshmukh Madam served as the coordinator for this workshop. According to the schedule, the workshop comprised numerous activities over the course of two days, totaling 12 hours of hands-on learning experience. Ultimately, the event concluded successfully.
